A system?

If you're asking yourself "what is a system", then an explanation might be required:

Plurality is the existence of multiple self-aware entities inside one physical brain.

You could think of a plural collective as a group of lifelong roommates, but with a body instead of an apartment.

more than one

We're multiple distinct folks sharing the same body. We each have our own sense of self, we can communicate between each other, and we have our own preferences, identity, personality, etc.

We often refer each other as headmates or constant companions.

If you spend enough time around us, you might notice all the little details that make each of us unique; be it through our writing style, our hobbies, our general demeanor, or even our voice.

Why? How are you a system?

That is partly a personal question, which we're not keen on answering out in the open. Nor do we owe a justification that we are.
